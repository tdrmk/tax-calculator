# Pre-Tax Deductions Reference

These deductions reduce your gross income before taxes are calculated.

## Retirement Contributions

| # | Deduction | 2025 Limit |
|---|-----------|------------|
| 1 | **Traditional 401(k)** | $23,500 ($31,000 if age 50+) |
| 2 | **Traditional IRA** | $7,000 ($8,000 if age 50+) — deductibility depends on income & workplace plan |
| 3 | **403(b)** | $23,500 ($31,000 if age 50+) |

## Health-Related

| # | Deduction | 2025 Limit |
|---|-----------|------------|
| 4 | **HSA (Health Savings Account)** | $4,300 self / $8,550 family ($1,000 catch-up if 55+) |
| 5 | **FSA (Flexible Spending Account)** | $3,300 |
| 6 | **Health insurance premiums** | Employer-sponsored, deducted pre-tax via payroll |
| 7 | **Dependent Care FSA** | $5,000 |

## Other Payroll Pre-Tax

| # | Deduction | Notes |
|---|-----------|-------|
| 8 | **Dental / Vision premiums** | If employer-sponsored |
| 9 | **Commuter / transit benefits** | $325/month parking, $325/month transit |
| 10 | **Life insurance premiums** | Employer-sponsored, up to $50K coverage is pre-tax |
